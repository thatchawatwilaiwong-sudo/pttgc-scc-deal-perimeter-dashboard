Thailand Petroleum & Petrochemical Industry Flow Dashboard

Full industry map

Poster-style full value-chain map for tracing feedstock movement through gas separation, refinery/splitter, olefins, aromatics, intermediates, polymers, specialties, converters and end markets. Use the company filter to highlight where each company sits and fade the rest.

How to read the full map

Company footprint

Start from feedstock on the left, then follow arrows into refinery/GSP products, monomers, intermediates, polymers and end markets. Tick a company in the left filter to see only where that company participates in the industry chain.

Capacity interpretation

Verify before use

Capacity shown is nameplate or public proxy, not utilization. Use it as a structural map first, then validate exact company-by-company capacity against latest annual report, APIC table or data-room source before drawing excess-capacity conclusions.
